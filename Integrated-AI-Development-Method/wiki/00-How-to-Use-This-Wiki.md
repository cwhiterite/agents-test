## How to Use Pattern Knowledge

### 1. Identify the Problem
Before reaching for a pattern, clearly define the problem you're solving.

### 2. Find Relevant Patterns
Use [Pattern-Index-by-Problem](15-Pattern-Index-by-Problem.md) to find patterns that address your problem.

### 3. Understand Before Applying
Study the pattern's:
- **Intent**: What problem does it solve?
- **Structure**: How is it organized?
- **Trade-offs**: What are the consequences?

### 4. Apply Appropriately
Adapt the pattern to your context, not the other way around.

## Key Principle

> **"Patterns are vocabulary, not goals."**

Patterns provide a shared language. Don't force-fit a pattern to a problem; let the problem suggest the appropriate solution.
